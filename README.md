# Safiri
Server- Express clone

This is safiri a simplified version of Express. Just playing around on building servers to understand what express does 

#### The following features have been implemented

1. Part 1: `app.listen(port)` and `app.get(url, callback)`

    Also support `req` and `res` inside route `callback` with
    1. `req.query`
    1. `res.send()`
    1. `res.json`
1. Part 2: `app.post(url, callback)`
1. Part 3: `app.use(routePrefix, callback)`
1. Part 4: `res.render(name, options)`
1. Part 5: `req.params`
1. Part 6: `next()` inside route
